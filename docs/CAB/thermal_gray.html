Thermal Spectrum: Grayscale

This is the spectrum of a 60W "soft white" incandescent lamp with HeNe red (633nm) and green (543 nm) laser spots for calibration. The camera was set to record in grayscale rather than color.

Compare this spectrum with that in "Thermal Spectrum: Color," in which the camera recorded in color. The line profile tool obains an almost identical spectrum in both, indicating that the camera and tool use similar video RGB-grayscale conversions.
