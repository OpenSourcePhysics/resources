Fluorescent Lamp, Warm White

Tracker Experiment

This is the spectrum of a fluorescent tube with HeNe red (633nm) and green (543 nm) laser spots for calibration. The manufacturer's rated color temperature for the tube is 3000K.

Analysis:

  1. How is the spectrum different from that of the 6300K fluorescent lamp? Do both lamps contain the same gas?
  2. How many different fluorescence peaks are identifiable? What is the wavelength of the brightest fluorescence peak?
  3. Which gas line(s) might be exciting this fluorescence peak?
  4. What is "color temperature" and why does the 6300K lamp have a higher color temperature than this one?
