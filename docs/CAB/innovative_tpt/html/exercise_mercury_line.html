Mercury Gas Lamp

Description
This is the spectrum of a Mercury gas lamp with HeNe red and green laser spots for calibration.

Procedure

  1. Open the Tracker exercise.
  2. Answer the questions below.

Analysis

  1. Measure the wavelengths of the three brightest lines in the Hg spectrum. Note particularly the bright line in the blue region. Hg also produces strong UV lines (not seen!).
