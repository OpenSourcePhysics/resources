Fluorescent Lamp: 6300K Color Temperature

Description
This is the spectrum of a fluorescent lamp with HeNe red and green laser spots for calibration. The manufacturer's rated color temperature for the tube is 6300K.

The fluorescent tube contains a gas and is coated on the inside with fluorescent dyes.

Procedure

  1. Open the Tracker exercise.
  2. Answer the questions below.

Analysis

  1. Identify the gas by measuring the wavelengths of the lines in the spectrum.
  2. How many different fluorescence peaks are identifiable? What is the wavelength of the brightest fluorescence peak?
  3. Which gas line(s) might be exciting this fluorescence peak?
