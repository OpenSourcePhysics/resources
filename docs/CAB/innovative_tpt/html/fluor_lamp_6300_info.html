Fluorescent Lamp, Cool White

Tracker Experiment

This is the spectrum of a fluorescent tube with HeNe red (633nm) and green (543 nm) laser spots for calibration. The manufacturer's rated color temperature for the tube is 6300K. The fluorescent tube contains a gas and is coated on the inside with fluorescent dyes.

Analysis:

  1. Identify the gas by measuring the wavelengths of the lines in the spectrum.
  2. How many different fluorescence peaks are identifiable? What is the wavelength of the brightest fluorescence peak?
  3. Which gas line(s) might be exciting this fluorescence peak?
