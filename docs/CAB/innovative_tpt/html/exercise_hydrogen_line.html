Hydrogen Gas Lamp

Description
This is the spectrum of a Hydrogen gas lamp with HeNe red and green laser spots for calibration.

Procedure

  1. Open the Tracker exercise.
  2. Answer the questions below.

Analysis

  1. Measure the visible wavelengths of H and estimate their uncertainty.
  2. Compare the measured values with those predicted by quantum mechanics. For which lines, if any, is the discrepancy significantly larger than the uncertainty?
  3. Why is the violet line relatively dim? What determines the relative intensities according to quantum mechanics? Why is the red line not the brightest in the image of the spectrum?
