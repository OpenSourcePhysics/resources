Angular Tethered

Tracker Experiment

This shows a collision between two air pucks. The puck that is initially at rest is tethered to a post and so is constrained to rotate in a circle. The other puck collides into it and bounces off.

Worksheet: a student worksheet is available at https://physlets.org/tracker/library/innovative_tpt/html/collisions_2D_angular_momentum_part1.html
